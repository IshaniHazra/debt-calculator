The key insight
Borrowing from an informal lender at 10% monthly costs 7–8x more than a bank loan over 2 years. Even a high-cost NBFC loan at 24% annual is dramatically cheaper than informal lending. If your family has informal debt, refinancing it into any formal loan — even at 30–36% — is an immediate financial win. The single most powerful move is to replace informal debt with formal debt as fast as possible.
